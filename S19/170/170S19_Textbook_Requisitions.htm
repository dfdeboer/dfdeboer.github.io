Comments:

DO NOT GET THE WRONG BOOK

The fourth edition is the wrong book for this section. Does the pattern on the cover of the book touch the edges of the cover? That's the wrong book!

INTERNATIONAL EDITIONS NOT SUPPORTED
International editions of the textbooks will not be supported by the instructor. Often the vender assures that an international edition is interchangeable with the domestic edition. It has never worked that way in Professor DDB's experience.

CDs, ACCESS CODES NOT NEEDED
Neither of the textbooks required for this course require access codes to Web pages or CD disks.

PAPERBACK EDITIONS RECOMMEMDED
The ISBN numbers provided above are for paperback editions. In Professor DDB's experience these paperback editions are of good construction. There is no practical advantage to purchasing theses in hard covers. This remark does not necessarily apply to other courses Professor DDB instructs.

NCEES-approved CALCULATOR OPTIONAL
Routinely using a calculator that you can take with you to the Fundamentals of Engineering Exam will improve your performance on the exam. In this course a calculator that can handle complex arithmetic and number base conversions is helpful. The approved list can be found here: http://ncees.org/exams/calculator/ Not all calculators on this list can do complex arithmetic and base conversions, but the TI-36X-Pro model can and is the choice of most engineering students at Dordt College.
