Further description of the optional 3rd Monday assignment

Optional, for up to 10 points of extra credit applied to your homework grade:

On Monday, 2/21, at 11:00 AM, in room S108, attend the "Third Monday—Engineering Panel" disscussion on the topic of engineering jobs. Then write up a reflection essay of between 500 and 1000 words. Submit your essay electronically as an MS-Word file in doc or docx format. The link to submit is near the very end of the EGR 204 courses@dordt page. Your essay must be submitted before class on Wednesday, 2/23. You must be present and on time at the event in S108 for your essay to qualify. Here are some suggestions for what to write about in your reflective essay:

Which of the panelists was most relevant to your situation and why? What did you learn that you did not already know and how will you respond to this new knowledge? Did Dordt's Dordt's engineering degree prepare the panelists for their careers in the ways you expected? Will it be as easy (or hard) for you to find a job as it was for the panelists? Pick one question or devise your own question and write an essay in response. The essay should not repeat the question but should state your response in a thesis sentence and defend the thesis with sentences and paragraphs
