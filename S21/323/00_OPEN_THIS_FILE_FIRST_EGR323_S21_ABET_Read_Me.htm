Contents of the EGR 323 Archive, Spring 2021 Semester

Electronics II
A junior-or-senior-level 3-hour course required for engineering majors in the electrical and computer concentrations. It is also optional for students in the bio and chemical concentrations. This course is offered in the spring of odd calendar years.
