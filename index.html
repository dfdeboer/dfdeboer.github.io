Office Hours, Spring 2020

Face-to-face meetings are by appointment only during the pandemic.
Phone calls need not be scheduled—just call 712- any time Monday through Saturday between 7:30 AM and 10 PM.

Prof. De Boer is over age 65 and is teaching only part-time now. Due to the pandemic he is minimizing the time he spends on campus to mostly class and lab hours. But making an appointment is welcomed and is easy. No appointment is needed to call on the phone.

Students have permission to telephone Prof. De Boer at home or at his office between 7:30 AM and 10 PM any day except Sunday. (Sunday is OK too if if the call is not about course work.) The phone numbers are: Home (try this first): 712-, and Office: 712-722-6245
