Before traveling (or walking) to visit me at my office, it is wise to call on the telphone and see if I'm in. If I do not answer my office phone you may try my home phone or leave a message at my office phone. If you are planning a few hours or more in advance, send e-mail to set up an appointment for a time that is mutually convenient. I set up appointments outside of office hours frequently.

Students also have permission to telephone me at home or office any day except Sunday between 8 AM and 10 PM (Sunday is OK too if if the call is not about course work). My office phone number is 712-722-6245. My home phone number is 712-.

I have a "virtual open door" policy. Any time I am in my office, I'd like to be available. Unless there is a "do not disturb" note on my office door, just knock—even if the door is physically closed. Similarly, if you are in a dorm or elsewhere and want to talk, just pick up your phone and call.
