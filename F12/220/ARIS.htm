INSTRUCTIONS FOR USING
McGRAW-HILL'S ARIS COURSEWARE SYSTEM
IN CONNECTION WITH EGR 220, PHYS 206

Professor De Boer primarily uses Dordt's "courses@dordt" course management system rather than McGraw-Hill's ARIS course management system. However students in his class may also create an account in ARIS and access the publisher's resources that coordinate with the textbook.

Start by clicking on the image of the textbook (above).

If this is your first time accessing ARIS you may need to create an account. Select the "register as a student" link and follow directions from there.

When you are logged into your ARIS account look for a tab or link to "Resources." You will be able to access all the textbook-related resources from that tab or link. Probably the most valuable resources you can find here are the solutions to the practice problems in the textbook.

Professor De Boer has not set up a "section enrollment code" for this course. Instead use the course page on the courses@dordt system to access course-specific information.
