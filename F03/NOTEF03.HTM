Notes on DDB's web pages

Note 1: To be sure your browser is giving you a view of the most
recently posted page, flush your cache.

In Netscape: press ctl-R after loading each page.
In Internet Explorer: press F5 after loading each page.

Either of the above steps will flush only the page you are viewing from
your cache, replacing that page in your cache with fresh information.
All other cached pages remain unchanged.


Note 2: To get back to the page you came from, use the "back" feature
of your browser. (Click the icon with a left-pointing arrow on it or
try alt-leftarrow on the keyboard.)

Note 3: Dordt College supports Internet Explorer 5.5 and 6.0 only. If you are
using Netscape and can't get the page to display correctly or if you are
getting out-of-date pages, try Internet Explorer instead.
