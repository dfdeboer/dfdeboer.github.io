***** Text Appendices (also available on "Canvas")

For access to the content on "Canvas" you must be enrolled in the course.

The one item on the DVD not available without the DVD and the textbook is the NI "Multisim Student Edition." However the Multisim software is not used at Dordt (we use the PSpice software). The similar NI "Multisim Academic Evaluation" 30-day trial is available free on the web at http://www.ni.com/multisim/try/ . The version included on the DVD with the textbook requires Windows Vista, Windows XP, or Windows 2000 SP3 or later, 32 bit versions only. Specifically, Multisim Student Edition 10.1 (on the DVD) is not specifically supported on Windows 7.

Students may only use an NCEES approved calculator for tests and the final exam. The approved list can be found here: http://www.ncees.org/exams/calculator-policy Complex number calculations are needed in this course. Thus a calculator model that can do complex number calculations is advantagious. The TI-36x Pro, Casio FX-115 ES, and HP 35s can do complex number calculations.
