Global Signal Generators

 

 

2001 Sweepable Function Generator

 

General Specifications:

 

  • Frequency Range: 1 Hz to 100 kHz in 5 decade ranges, push button selectable; 50 increment 10:1 dial calibrated +/- 5% of setting at       10Hz, 100Hz, 1kHz, 10kHz.
  • Waveforms: Sine, Triangle, Square.
  • TTL Square: rise and fall times under 25 nsec.
  • Sweep Range: 100:1 max; 10:1 linear range; 0 to +/-10V Sweep input, input impedance 30,000 ohms.

·        DC Offset: switch selectable; +/_5V into an open circuit; max offset +/-10V at Hi output and +/-1V at Lo into open circuit, +/-5V at Hi and +/-5V at Lo into 600 ohm load.

 

Front Panel Controls:

 

  • The Range Switches act as multipliers for the frequency vernier.
  • The Frequency vernier acts as a continuous fine tuning adjustment for setting the output frequency.
  • The Amplitude Control is a linear potentiometer that varies the DC-coupled level up to 10 Volts no load, and 5 Volts with a 600 Ohm load.
  • The DC Offset Switch offsets the centerline of the waveform at the HI output +/-5 Volts no load, +/- 2.5 Volts at 600 Ohms.

 

2003 Synthesized Function Generator

Jump to operating instructions

 

General Specifications:

 

  • Frequency Range: 0.001Hz to 1.6MHz
  • Frequency Resolution: 10 digits, 0.001Hz
  • Frequency Accuracy: +/-10ppm per year
  • Waveforms: sine, square, triangle, positive ramp, negative ramp
  • Waveform Purity:

a) Sine Wave Harmoic and Non harmonic Distortion better than 40dB below fundamental at full output.

b) Triangle Wave Nonlinearity < 1% up to 100 KHz

c)      Ramp Nonlinearity < 1% up to 100kHz

 

  • Total Harmonic distortion: < 1% over full range

 

  • Output Amplitude

a)     Sine, Square, Triangle and Ramps 5mV – 20V p-p inot open circuit 2.5 mVp-p into 50 ohms digitally controlled, +/-3% accuracy

b)     TTL Square Wave 2.5V high, 0.4V low

 

  • Output Resolution:

a)     0.1V, 2.0V to 20V

b)     10mV, 0.2V to 2V

c)      1mV, 5mV to 200mV

 

  • Output Offset:

a)     +10V to –10V open circuit

b)     +5V to –5V into 50 ohm

c)      +/- 3 %

 

  • Attenuator, 3 ranges: 0dB, -20dB, -40dB +/-3%

 

  • Output Impedance: 50 ohms

 

  • Operational modes:

a)     continuous, triggered, gated, sweep, hoop, burst

b)     Trigger gate: externally controlled

c)      Sweep: lin/log, 10ms to 1000s internally generated

d)     Hop: up to 16 preset frequency can be programmed into memory.  Time duration settable in 1 micro-second to 1000 micro-second minimum increments at each frequency.  Minimum duration is 100 micro-second, maximum duration is 1000 seconds.

e)     Burst: During hop mode a new frequency can be externally triggered or the output can be gated on/off for a preset number of cycles.

 

  • Tuning: tuning knob for selecting functions and setting values.

 

  • Communications port: STD RS232 interface with 9 pin “D” connector at rear panel

 

Operating Instructions For Continuous, Gated and Triggered Modes:  (See manual for operating instructions in other modes)

 

  • Setting Frequency:

1) From top menu press “Freq”.

            2) To enter a completely new frequency use the keyboard, the new frequency takes effect when you press “enter”

3) To change an existing frequency press the “+/-“ key tomove the cursor under the digit to be changed and rotate the tuning knob.

    As the knob is rotated the new frequencies take effect immediately.  Press “Enter” when finished.

 

  • Setting Output Amplitude and Offset:

1) From the top menu press “Output”.  The display shows the output voltage and amplitude. 

2) Set as previously explained in “Setting Frequency”

 

  • Setting Waveforms:

1) From the top menu press “Wave”.

2) rotate the tuning knob to select a waveform, press “Enter” when finished.

3) See manual for exceptions
