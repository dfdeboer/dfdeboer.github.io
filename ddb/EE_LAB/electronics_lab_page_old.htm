Safely Guidelines

Instructions on lab safety are posted on the wall in the lab near the telephone.

 

Wear safety goggles (and make sure your neighbors are also) at least whenever you are:

 

1.      Soldering

2.      Drilling

3.      Cutting or stripping wire
