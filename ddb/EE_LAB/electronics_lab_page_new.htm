GUIDE TO DORDT COLLEGE'S ELECTRONICS LAB

SAFETY FIRST

Wear safety glasses and make sure others nearby do to when:
1) Soldering
2) Drilling
3) Cutting/Stripping wire
4) No list can be comprehensive. Use your good judgement.

Instructions on emergency procedures are posted on the wall to the right of the whiteboard, near the telephone.
