Past courses instructed by Professor De Boer
Click a link to access information for a particular semester.

In the list below, "U" = "summer semester", "F" = "fall semester" and "S" = "spring semester."
Example: "F09" means the "fall semester of 2009."

Prior to May 13, 2019, "Dordt University" was known as, "Dordt College"
Web pages for courses taught prior to U19 will show the institution's name as, "Dordt College"
The change from College to University was a change in name only.
The ownership, administration, accreditation status and so forth was unchanged.

Professor De Boer started using web pages for course management in S02.
If a date prior to S02 is shown but it is not a link it means the course was instructed by Professor De Boer during that semester but there is nothing related available online.
In the Dordt College Catalog for 2000–2001 several courses were renumbered. Parenthetic remarks
below such as,"same courses as. . ." reflect this renumbering.

If you cannot find what you are looking for here, you are encouraged to e-mail professor De Boer to ask about it, but there is no assurance the information for older courses has been archived.
