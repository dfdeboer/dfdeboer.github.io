Contents of the EGR 204 "Course Binder" for ABET, 2023

Introduction to Microprocessors and Digital Circuits
A sophomore-level course required for engineering majors in the electrical and computer concentrations. It is also optional for students in the biomedical concentration. Since 2017 This course has been offered in fall semesters of odd calendar years.
