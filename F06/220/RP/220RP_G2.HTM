Grading Part Two of the Research Paper

"A"
All deficiencies in the thesis statement and bibliography have been fixed. (See the grading rubric for part one and your graded copy of part one.) The outline includes an introduction and a conclusion each of which contain content as specified in the booklet "How to Write a Lab Report." The outline clearly shows the major headings you will use and it shows how the thesis will be defended and how the bibliography will be used.

"B"
One or two of these problems: Thesis still does not meet the specification of part one. Bibliography still does not meet the specifications of part one. Outline does not contain an introduction and conclusion. Outline does not show how the thesis will be defended. Outline does not show how the bibliography will be used.


"C"
More than two of the problems described above although a good- faith effort shows. --OR-- inadequate bibliography showing insufficient improvement from part one.


"D"
(This grade will not be given.)


"F"
Anything less than a "C"

(Evidence for "Good faith" effort includes the logical relationships between elements of the outline, showing thought and consideration of the subject and supporting the thesis.)
