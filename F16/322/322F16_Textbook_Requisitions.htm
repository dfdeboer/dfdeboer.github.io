TEXTBOOK'S WEB SITE
FYI this textbook has an accompanying web site that will be useful to this course. Several appendices to the textbook and other resources are hosted at the textbook's web site. See http://www.sedrasmith.org

INTERNATIONAL EDITION NOT SUPPORTED
International editions of the textbook will not be supported by the instructor. Some book vendors are devious and will sell an international edition under the domestic ISBN number, although usually somewhere in the description of the product they admit that they are selling an "international edition." International editions are cobbled together from older editions of the textbook and sold at a discount, often with the assurance that they are virtually interchangeable with the domestic edition. They are not. Also note that most paperback editions are international editions. If the word "international" appears anywhere in the description or in photos describing the book, buyer beware.

ELECTRONIC EDITION NOT SUPPORTED
Open book tests are not open computer or open Nook or open Kindle etc. tests. Thus an electronic edition of the textbook will not be allowed at a test. Students need a hardcopy version of the textbook.

NCEES-approved CALCULATOR REQUIRED
Students may only use an NCEES approved calculator for tests and the final exam. The approved list can be found here: http://ncees.org/exams/calculator/ Most students choose a TI-36X-Pro model. Further discussion can be found here: http://blogs.dordt.edu/ddeboer/2012/05/30/what-calculator-is-best-for-engineering/
