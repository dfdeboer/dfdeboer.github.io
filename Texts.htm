Professor De Boer's Textbook Requisitions

(Last updated on Tuesday, May 2, 2018)

The links below will show you which textbooks Professor De Boer has ordered for each of his courses. The links are to the forms that De Boer sent to the Dordt College Bookstore for each course that he has submitted such a form. (Some links in the requisition forms may be dead.) If you want to order a textbook online or by mail order you may use these requisition forms to identify the particular editions (and ISBN numbers) that you need.

Be sure to look for the comments section near the end of each requisition form. If there are comments they might be important.

Use your Browser's back-arrow icon or press the alt-leftarrow key to get back to this page from one of the requisition pages linked below. These requisitions sometimes load slowly and without images on some browsers since I've linked them out of their original context. Just be patient. What you need will load in a minute or two.

Prices, if shown, are for books purchased or rented at the Dordt College Bookstore. If a price is not shown for a certain type of book (e.g. no price for a used book), it means that Prof. De Boer does not have information about that book. You may also check the bookstore's booklist, however it usually contains less informaition for Prof. De Boer's courses than the requisition forms linked below.
