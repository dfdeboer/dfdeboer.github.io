Proof
Capacitive Reactive Power is Always Negative.

Capacitive reactance is XC = –1/ωC.

Assuming that frequency, ω, and capacity, C, are always positive, which is conventionally the case, then capacitive reactance is always negative.

Now the resulting ractive power is Q = |VRMS|2/XC.

Thus since XC is always negative, capacitive reactive power is also always negative.
