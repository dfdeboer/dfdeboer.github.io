In addition, there are the following errata:

Page 27. Problem 1.34 part (b)
Change "(b) the average power per hour"
to "(b) the average power over the given 24 hour interval."

        The average power per hour (at each hour) is obvious from the given graph and is
        not the intended answer. (Posted 9/20/2010.)

Page 164. Problem 4.20
Correct the problem as shown below. The corrctions include the addition of labels a and b at the top and bottom nodes, the addition of a dashed box around the circuit with terminals extending out of the dashed box, and some re-wording of the question.



        Without the changes shown there is no resonable way to apply the concept of
        equivalency in order to reduce the circuit in a meaningful way.
        (posted 9/08/2010)

Page A-78. Answer to Problem 4.23
Change "2 A, 32 W" to "3 A, 72 W."
        (Posted 9/13/2010. This erratum is also noted in the author's list)

Page A-78. Answer to Problem 4.47
Change "4.176 A" to "4.167 A."
        (Posted 9/13/2010)

Page A-78. Answer to Problem 5.5.
Change "0.9999990" to "0.999990"
(Five nines, not six of them.)
        (Posted 9/15/2010)
