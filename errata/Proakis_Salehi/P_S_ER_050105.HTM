Page 31, Example 2.1.7
   Replace "A/2" with just "A" in four places.  

                                              
Page 68, Equation below the line that starts "To show. . ."
   Replace "dt" with "df".

   deviation are incorrectly given.  Replace "Hz/v" with "V".
