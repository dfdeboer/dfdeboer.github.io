Comments:

HARDCOPY REQUIRED
Electronic editions of the textbook will not be allowed for use on open book tests or on the final exam. If you order the electronic edition you will have to print pages to bring to the tests with you.

INTERNATIONAL EDITION NOT SUPPORTED
International editions of the textbook will not be supported by the instructor. Often the vender assures that the international edition is virtually interchangeable with the domestic edition. It has never worked that way in Professor DDB's experience.

PAPERBACK EDITION NOT SUPPORTED
Paperbacks of this textbook are practically always international editions. If you know the paperback is the domestic edition, then it is OK.

EACH STUDENT NEEDS A PERSONAL COPY
Some tests and the final exam in this course will be "open book." Each student will simultaniously need access to a textbook. Textbooks may not be shared during a test or during the final exam.

NCEES-approved CALCULATOR REQUIRED
Students may only use an NCEES approved calculator for tests and the final exam. The approved list can be found here: http://ncees.org/exams/calculator/ Most students choose a TI-36X-Pro model. Further discussion can be found here: https://dfdeboer.github.io/BLOG/Archive/What_Calculator.htm
