Contents of the EGR 322 Archive, Fall 2020 Semester

Electronics I
A junior-or-senior-level 3-hour course required for engineering majors in the electrical and computer concentrations. It is also optional for students in the bio and chemical concentrations. This course is offered in the fall of Even calendar years.
